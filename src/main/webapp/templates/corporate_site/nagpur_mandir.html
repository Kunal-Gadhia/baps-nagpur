BAPS Shri Swaminarayan Mandir,Nagpur

Welcome to the BAPS Swaminarayan Mandir,Nagpur.A unique complex that beautifully showcases Hindu art, architecture, wisdom, heritage, values, and spirituality. The mandir is an abode of Bhagwan Swaminarayan (1781-1830 CE), and his ideal devotee Aksharbrahma Gunatitanand Swami, radiating peace, beauty, joy, and divinity.inspired by Pramukh Swami Maharaj, the mandir was completed in only 5 years and inaugurated on 7 October 2013.
